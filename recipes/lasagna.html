Lasagna recipe

Juicy, cheesy AND delicious lasagna

For this recipe you will need around 2 hours of free time and a will to create good food

Ingredients:

  • Pasta for lasagna
  • ground beef
  • Basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Make a sauce with the meat and tomato sauce
  2. Add a layer of pasta
  3. Put sauce on pasta
  4. Put cheese on the sauce
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until the tray is full
