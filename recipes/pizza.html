Pizza recipe

Juicy, cheesy AND delicious lasagna

Classic italian style pizza ready to be made at your own home

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Shape the pizza dough
  2. Add a layer of tomato sauce
  3. Grind cheese on sauce
  4. Add Pepperoni
  5. Put the pizza in the oven at the highest heat
  6. Once the pizza is done put basil leaves on top and serve!
