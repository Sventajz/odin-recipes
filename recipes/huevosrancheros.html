Huevos rancheros recipe

A healty and delicous breakfast worthy of your day

This recipes doesn't require a lot of effor or time. A perfect meal to start your day!

Ingredients:

  • 2 torillas
  • Eggs
  • Salsa rosa
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Heat tortillas until crispy
  2. Make sunny side up eggs
  3. Put eggs on tortillas
  4. Drench the eggs with sauce and cheese
  5. Serve and enjoy
